sus

/sʌs/
adjective
  1. giving the impression that something is questionable or dishonest; suspicious.
    'it's a little sus that he seems to know exactly how to play this game'
  2. relating to or denoting a law under which a person could be arrested on suspicion of having committed an offence.
    'the sus law'

noun
suspicion of having committed a crime.
'he was picked up on sus'
