Replit

Company · replit.com
Replit, formerly Repl.it, is a San Francisco-based start-up and an online IDE. Its name comes from the acronym REPL, which stands for "read–evaluate–print loop". Amjad Masad, Faris Masad, and Haya Odeh co-founded the company in 2016. Wikipedia
Founded: 2016
Number of employees: 30
Founders: Amjad Masad, Haya Odeh, and Faris Masad
